About Lildney

Lildney is a talented Zambian R&B/Soul artist known for his soulful voice and authentic storytelling. Born and raised in Lusaka, Zambia, Lildney's music is heavily influenced by his African heritage and his passion for soulful melodies.

With a career spanning over 5 years, Lildney has established himself as one of Zambia's most promising young artists. His music has gained popularity not only in Zambia but also in other parts of Africa and beyond.

Lildney's music is a fusion of R&B, Soul, and Afrobeats, with lyrics that speak to love, heartbreak, and the struggles of everyday life. His soulful voice and captivating stage presence have earned him a loyal fan base and critical acclaim from music enthusiasts.
